# Estate Maintenance

[Engineering Unit](https://enggunit.iitm.ac.in/) (EU) at IITM is responsible for estate maintenance (academic & residential). 

All complaints to EU must be registered using the IIT Madras EU CMS app. This may be downloaded from Google Play Store/ Apple Store.  
For larger jobs, a request letter is needed. The letter is addressed to the Assistant Executive Engineer for both Electrical and Civil Maintenance.

| Service                | Number                                          | Typical job profile                                                                                                                                                             |
|------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Civil Maintenance      | 8192                                            | Repairs, internal plumbing, carpentry, painting, debris disposal, and other civil related works                                                                                                     |
| Electrical Maintenance | 8193                                            |                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| Water Supply           | 8190                                            | External plumbing operations (outside building), drainage, removal of blockages in sewer lines                                                                                                              |
| Horticulture and Public health-related         | 8194 | Fogging operations of mosquito control, Desilting of storm water drains, Maintenance of lawns, gardens, trees, pruning of branches, and other horticultural operations on campus, Wildlife emergencies |
