# Funding from Alumni, Philanthropists & Corporates

Office of Alumni & Corporate Relations (ACR), headed by Dean (ACR), connects with alumni network, philanthropists, and corporates (Corporate Social Responsibility - CSR funds) raise awareness about the Institute, with the aim of acquiring support, grants and donations for the Institute.

Office of ACR: Location- 2<sup>nd</sup> floor, ICSR Building. 8071, email- [deanacroffice@iitm.ac.in](mailto:deanacroffice@iitm.ac.in), Website- [https://acr.iitm.ac.in/](https://acr.iitm.ac.in/)

The main responsibility of this office is to reach out to Alumni, Philanthropists, and Corporates to raise awareness about the Institute, with the aim of acquiring support, grants and donations for the Institute. 
Typical causes for which office raises funds include:
-	Large infrastructure development
-	Common research facilities
-	Faculty fellowships & chair professorships
-	CSR funds to support socially-relevant projects that fulfil one or more of the 17 United Nations Sustainability Development Goals.
-	Support the students in the form of scholarship/ aid and awards/ prizes. 

While preparing a proposal:
-	identify a cause of interest to individual/ corporates to increase the chances of acceptance. 
-	the proposal should address the requirements of a larger section of the Institute/ society

The office of A&CR has a Proposal Development and a Marketing Team to help the faculty in the creation of a suitable, attractive proposal complete with technical details, diagrams, photographs, end-result/impact, budget, time-lines, etc.
