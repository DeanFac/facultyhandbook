## Faculty Fellowships and Endowed Chairs
Faculty Fellowships and Endowed Chairs have been set up to recognize outstanding faculty in various stages of their career (Early/ Mid/ Senior) for their achievements in research, collaborating with industry in R&D, innovation, and implementation of state-of-the-art solutions. These are awarded based on the domain of research identified by the donors. Announcements are made for new applications for the fellowship/ endowed chairs on the completion of the tenure of each occupant (usually 5 years). The selection is by an internal committee of senior professors. 

## Institute Chairs
Chair Professorships are awarded to Professors of IIT Madras who have distinguished themselves and been recognized by their peers for their research and/or technology development. The Chair Professorships are through selection by an external Peer Review Committee. 

## Industry & Ministry Chairs
The Industry and Ministry Chairs are instituted by various ministries or industries to support faculty working in specific domains. The selection will happen as spelt out in the respective MoUs.
