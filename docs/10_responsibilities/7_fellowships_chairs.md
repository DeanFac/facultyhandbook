## Faculty Fellowships and Early Career Chairs
Announcements are made for new applications for the fellowship/ endowed chairs on the completion of the tenure of each occupant (usually 5 years). Fellowship includes Rs. 25,000/- per month.

## Institute Chairs
Chair Professorships are awarded to Professors of IIT Madras who have distinguished themselves and been recognized by their peers for their research and/or technology development. The award is for a period of 5 years followed by a review and possible extension.
