# Authorized Travel Agents
As per the Government of India order, all official air travel tickets (domestic and international) must be purchased using the services of three Authorized Travel Agents/: 
	
1. [Balmer Lawrie & Company Limited](https://govemp.balmerlawrietravelapp.com/)- Travel desk at IC&SR (Ph: 9709; Email: iit.implant@balmerlawrie.com)
	
2. Ashok Travels - attchennai100@gmail.com
	
3. Indian Railways Catering and Tourism Corporation Ltd. (IRCTC)

# Booking with Balmer Lawrie & Co. Ltd.

Faculty members who are not availing the services of the Balmer Lawrie Desk at ICSR may also use the Balmer Lawrie self-booking option for travel. Self-booking can be done either through the general IIT Madras account or through individual accounts registered as Government of India users.

* The general IITM account may be used by those who do not have individual accounts to login to [the website](https://ssbt.balmerlawrietravelapp.com/Account/UserLogin). For login details, contact ICSR at [smstrategy-icsr@imail.iitm.ac.in](smstrategy-icsr@imail.iitm.ac.in).

* An individual IITM account can be created using [this link](https://govemp.balmerlawrietravelapp.com/Traveller/ICISTravellerRegistration).
