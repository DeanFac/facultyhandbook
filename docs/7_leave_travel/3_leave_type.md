# Various types of Leaves and their details

*Non-working days can be prefixed/ suffixed to CL, EL, vacation.
* Unused CL, SCL, lapses on Dec 31 each year & cannot be carried forward.


| Type of <br> Leave | Duration <br> in days  | Purpose & Remarks  |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Casual Leave (CL) | 8 / yr| • **For personal/ any other reasons** <br> • Cannot exceed 8 days at a stretch including prefix/ suffix. <br>  • Can be taken for half-day. <br> • Can be combined with SCL/Vacation but not with any other leave.|
| Special Casual Leave (SCL)   | 15 / yr | • **Conferences/ Workshops (with/without presenting paper) <br>  • Committee meetings not treated as ‘on-duty’ <br>  •PhD viva elsewhere**| 
| ICSR SCL | 6 / month during semester;  15 each during winter & summer  | • **For travel related to project & continuing education.** <br>  • Can be accumulated to a maximum of 30 days. Accumulated leave can be availed only during summer/ winter.  |
| Professional Visit | Min 30- Max 45 | • **Paid position during summer break** <br> • Separate paperwork has to be done (admin 1), not part of workflow.  |
| On Duty  (MoE/ Institute) | As sanctioned by Director |• **For representing Institute to Serve on committees**<br> • **Specific academic activities at the insistence of MoE, AICTE, etc. with/without Institute finance.** |
| Earned Leave  (EL) | Max 180 at a stretch |• **No reasons to be provided** <br> • EL can be accrued **only** by working during vacation: 2 days of working during vacation = 1 day of EL. <br>15 days of EL is credited to the employee on Jan 1 & Jul 1 every year.   <br>• Unused EL can be **encashed** anytime (lifetime cap of 300 days). 'Encashed' &rArr; convert EL to money. 1 EL equivalent to 1 day's salary reckoned as of the time of encashment. <br>• **In addition to the 300 days cap mentioned above, up to 10 days of EL may be encashed for every Leave Travel Concession (LTC) availed, capped at 60 days for lifetime.** <br>•  All encashed ELs, except at the time of retirement, are subject to income tax. <br> • Maximum EL balance that can be carried forward is 300 days \+ 15 days credit received on Jan/July. <br> • Can be combined with any leave except CL. <br> • Intervening holidays will be treated as EL. |
| Half-Pay Leave (HPL)  | 10 for every 6 months | **Medical grounds / personal reasons** |
| Commuted Leave (CmL)  | Depends on medical condition. Max ( along with EL, if taken) 240. |• **Medical grounds (based on medical certificate endorsed by IITM CMO)** <br> • CmL is with full pay. <br> • For 1 day of CmL, 2 days of HPL deducted. <br> • ‘Fit to join duty’ certificate by IITM CMO to be submitted while rejoining. |
| Sabbatical Leave  (SL)  | Min 4 months. Max 1 year   | • **Temporary R&D/ academic assignments in other universities or research institutions or industries, availing fellowships, writing books etc.** The offer letter from the host organization has to be produced.<br> • Eligibility- Continuous service for 3 years after becoming a regular faculty. <br> • 2 months SL credited per year of continuous service. <br> • Since the Board of Governors (BoG) of IITM is the SL granting body, apply at least 4 months in advance. <br> •During SL: full salary from IITM; retention of quarters. <br>  • Host institution cannot give regular salary during SL. The letter from the host institution should clearly indicate that you are only being paid an honorarium or living allowance. <br>  •  BoG may permit adding up to 60 (or 120\) days of EL/ HPL/ SCL/ ICSR SCL if SL is less than 6 (or more than 6\) months. <br>  • Have to furnish a bond to serve the Institute for one year on return to duty.  <br>  • Post SL for 4/ 4-8/ 8-12 months, min. window of 1/2/3 years, respectively, before taking the next SL.  |
| Extra- ordinary Leave (EOL) | 1 yr per 5 yrs of service <br>  Max 4 yrs, limited to 2 yrs at a time | • **Short/Long term assignments for R&D activities/ Fellowship/ Visiting positions elsewhere/ due to Sickness (Medical Certificate required)/ personal situations** <br> • No pay or allowance from IITM. Can get paid from host organization. <br> • Minimum service of 5 yrs (3 years on a case-to-case basis) before availing EOL. <br>• **Sabbatical & EOL entitlement are linked.** |
| Maternity Leave   | Maternity - 6 Months (for up to 2 surviving children)<br> Miscarriage - 6 Weeks | • **Maternity/ Adoption/ Miscarriage.** Medical Certificate should be produced.<br> • Will not be debited in leave account. <br> • Can be combined with any other leave except CL. |
| Paternity Leave   | 15 (including Sat / Sun & intervening holidays)- for up to 2 surviving children | • **Paternity/ Adoption** <br> • From 15 days before to 6 months after the date of delivery of the child.  15 days from the date of valid adoption of a child. It will lapse if the leave is not availed within this period.  |
| Child Care Leave  (CCL) | 730 during the service till 2nd surviving child reaches the age of 18\.    Min period of 5 days  | • **Child Care** <br> • Eligibility - Women or widowed/divorced father <br> • Not more than 3 times in a calendar year. <br> • 100% of salary for the first 365 days & 80% of the salary for the next 365 days.  |
| Vacation (it can be suffixed / prefixed with EL/HPL) | 60 days within the windows declared by IITM in summer & winter | • 1 EL credit for every 2 days of vacation not taken.  <br> • New Faculty members get 2 days vacation per 12 days of service in the Academic year within the windows declared by IITM. <br> •  Travel using SCL, ICSR-SCL etc is considered as working during vacation. |

# Types of Visits  
*  Brief – Less than 30 days  
* Short - 30-90 days  
* Long - Above 90 days
