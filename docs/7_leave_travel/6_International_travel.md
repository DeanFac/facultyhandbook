# Funding Sources

* **[CPDA](https://admin.iitm.ac.in/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/Per-Diem-Rule.pdf)** (Cumulative Professional Development Allowance) is meant for attending conferences/ workshops. For CPDA related queries, contact: 8249.

* **PCF** (Personal Contingency Fund) can also be utilized for attending international conference. In such a case, 50% of the cost is covered by ICSR. Email Dean ICSR before travel. 

* **ANRF** (Anusandhan National Research Foundation) provides funding under [International Travel Support (ITS) Scheme](https://anrfonline.in/ANRF/its) for presenting a research paper in an international scientific event (conference, seminar, workshop etc.). Support is also provided to young scientists (age limit below 35 years as on date of start of the event) for attending training programmes, Short-term schools and Workshops (max duration 21 days). 
Funding includes:

   - Economy class air-fare by shortest route, airport-tax and visa fees
   - Only for young scientists (<35 yrs on the date of start of event): Registration fee as per actual or Rs. 50,000/- whichever is less 

    Submit applications 60-90 days in advance (early or late submissions are not accepted). 

# Accommodation and Per Diem

**Number of days eligible for Accommodation & Per Diem -** Conference/ event days  + 1 day before  + 1 day after.

* **Accommodation charges** as per approved panel of hotel rates by cities (typically, \\${{ site.data.variables.acco }}) or actuals whichever is less. 

* Per diem Allowance through **Project Funds**- \\${{ site.data.variables.usuk }} for USA, UK, Canada, Australia, France, Belgium, Hong Kong, China, South Korea, etc.; {{ site.data.variables.perdiemother }} for other countries. Travel via project funds, see [ICSR Travel norms](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1uwmMav2ADUdBHme1G2g3r69wfIJ_085P/view?usp=drive_link).
